Hi, I'm Tyler. I have always loved learning outside of class, in fact, I taught myself how to make this website reverse-engineering an open-source template I found online. Growing up in Dallas in a single parent household, I made the move to LA to broaden my horizons and treat the moment as a trampoline into a better future for myself. I studied engineering and writing at the University of Southern California, until I graduated in May 2020. Some of my favorite memories include going stargazing with our astronomy professor in the Anza Borrego desert, grilling burgers with my fraternity to handout to the homeless every friday night, and learning how to paddleboard for the first time outside of Santa Barbara (and falling at least 6 times in the first hour). I felt incredibly supported by a group of young men that helped me find purpose, belonging and resilience as an individual and as a leader.

I enjoy the outdoors and helping people learn. I love fishing, video-editing, off-roading and surfing, but I'm still learning the latter. Including everyone and learning from those around me is important. I'm also excited for this next chapter in my life. Thanks for checking out my portfolio!
